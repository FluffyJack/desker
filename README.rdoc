*Desker*

A site to find, locate and share desk space with like minded people around you

*Install*

After downloading, run

bundle install

*Create your database*

rake db:migrate

*Load seed data*

rake db:seed

*Features*

Find desks in your area via geolocation

View Google Maps, Street View of potential spaces to work

Review System, with desks rated on performance by users

Bootstrapped
